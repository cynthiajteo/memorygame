Memory Game

Welcome!

This is a game to test your memory! All you have to do is open one card at a time and remember the image and match it with another card that has the exact image.

Click on the Hint! button if you need some help. It will show all the images for 2 seconds before flipping over. Be warned - you will lose 5 HP if you click on it!

There are 3 levels of difficulty for you to choose from: Easy, Medium, Hard.

Easy: 10 cards with 5 pairs of different images
Medium: 16 cards with 8 pairs of different images
Hard: 24 cards with 12 pairs of different images

GOOD LUCK AND HAVE FUN!

Scoring System

Combo System:
Increases after every consecutive match
Resets to 0 if miss a match and - 5HP

Score System:
Combo: 0 - add 10 points
Combo: 1 - add 10 points
Combo: 2 - add 20 points + 10HP
Combo: 3 & above - add 30 points + 10HP
